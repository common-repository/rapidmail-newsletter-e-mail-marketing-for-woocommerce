== Installation ==

= Install rapidmail WooCommerce integration =

**Log in to your rapidmail account (optional, but recommended)**.

1. Click on your **username** in the top right corner.
2. Click on **Integrations** in the dropdown menu
3. Click on the **Create a new integration** button
4. Follow the wizard and please watch the tutorial video
5. Click on the **Log into my system and install plugin** button (you will be redirected to your WordPress admin's login).

**Log in to your WordPress admin**.

1. If you have not installed the rapidmail plugin yet, please watch the help video first.
2. Click on **Plugins** then on **Add new** in the left sidebar.
3. Type **rapidmail** in the search field
4. Search for the rapidmail integration for WooCommerce and click **Install Now**.
5. After the installation is successful, click **Activate**.

**Connect to rapidmail**.

1. Click on **WooCommerce** in the left sidebar then on **Rapidmail** in the menu item below.
2. Click on the button **Create a new connection to rapidmail**.
3. Log in to your rapidmail account (if you are not already logged in).
4. The connection to WooCommerce will be established automatically and the rapidmail wizard will guide you through the last steps
5. Done, your contacts are synchronized, and your integration is fully functional!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is importing my recipients into rapidmail legally secure? =

Yes, with the rapidmail plugin for WooCommerce you can display a consent to your newsletter in your store as a checkbox at **Advanced Plugin Settings**. With this option customers can sign up for your newsletter. After the import, contacts who have signed up for your newsletter will be automatically marked with the corresponding tag in your recipient list.

= Can I send newsletters with attachments with rapidmail? =

Yes, with rapidmail you can easily add a file attachment to your newsletters. Whether product catalog, info brochure, company portrait or promotions - there are no limits to your ideas!

= Are rapidmail newsletters optimized for mobile devices? =
Yes, rapidmail newsletters are optimized for all types of end devices: With numerous templates in Responsive Design, you can create mobile-optimized newsletters in just a few minutes that are well received on all devices!

= Can I send individual and personalized newsletters with rapidmail? =

With rapidmail and the rapidmail newsletter plugin for WordPress you can easily give your mailings a personal touch. Data of your recipients, such as name and gender, are automatically transferred to your rapidmail recipient list - you only have to insert them into your mailing with a placeholder.

= Do my newsletters reach my recipients? =

The deliverability of your newsletters is one of the most important factors of your email marketing campaigns. To ensure that your mailings arrive, we are a member of the Certified Senders Alliance (CSA). This means that we are on the whitelist for preferred delivery with all major German mail providers (gmx, web.de, yahoo, and many more) and are certified by the CSA for reliable and secure delivery of newsletters.
